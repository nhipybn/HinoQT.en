Founded in 2015 in Hanoi, Hino Systech Vietnam is the Southeast Asia branch of Hino Systech Corporation (Japan). We specialize in control panels and centralized electrical monitoring systems (SCADA).

In line with digital transformation and smart operation management, Hino Systech Vietnam develops and deploys the OCC – Operation Control Center as a centralized operation platform. OCC consolidates and standardizes operational data from multiple plants or distributed projects into a single center, enabling real-time visibility, optimized O&M costs, enhanced asset safety, and proactive, transparent governance.

Operational Barriers in Solar Power Plant Portfolios

High labor cost burden

Maintaining technical teams at each small-scale project significantly increases O&M costs and directly impacts profitability.

Inefficient O&M resource utilization

Dispatching technicians without clear fault identification results in wasted time and unnecessary travel costs.

Lack of data consistency

Difficulty in consolidating generation reports and reconciliation when data is scattered across multiple platforms.

Asset security risks

Rooftop and industrial park projects face a high risk of material loss without 24/7 monitoring.

Hino OCC – Centralized Operation Center Solution

Hino Systech provides solutions for building a Central Operation Control Room, transforming the management model from decentralized to centralized.

Whether projects are located in factories or energy fields, operational data is continuously transmitted to enterprise headquarters in real time.

Management gains accurate visibility into asset conditions without the need for on-site presence.

Challenges in Operating Solar Power Portfolios

High labor costs

Maintaining on-site teams at each project increases O&M expenses.

O&M resource inefficiency

Unclear fault diagnosis leads to wasted dispatch time and cost.

Fragmented data

Operational data scattered across multiple platforms.

Asset security risks

Lack of 24/7 monitoring increases theft and loss risks.

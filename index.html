Hino Systech Vietnam, established in 2015 in Hanoi, is the Southeast Asia branch of Hino Systech Japan. We specialize in control panels and centralized power monitoring systems (SCADA).

Following digital transformation and smart operation management, Hino Systech Vietnam develops and deploys the OCC – Operation Control Center as a centralized operation platform. OCC consolidates and standardizes operational data from multiple plants into a single center, enabling real-time visibility, optimized O&M costs, enhanced asset safety, and transparent governance.

Operational Challenges in Solar Power Plant Portfolios

High labor costs

Maintaining on-site technicians at each small project significantly increases O&M expenses and reduces profitability.

Inefficient O&M resource allocation

Dispatching technicians without clear fault diagnosis leads to wasted time and travel costs.

Fragmented data

Difficulty in consolidating generation reports when data is scattered across multiple platforms.

Asset security risks

Rooftop and industrial projects face material loss risks without 24/7 monitoring.

Hino OCC – Centralized Operation Solution

Hino Systech delivers centralized control room solutions, transforming management from decentralized to centralized.

Regardless of plant location, operational data is transmitted to headquarters in real time.

Management can accurately monitor asset status without being physically on site.
